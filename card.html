The Giant Panda

The average panda spends 14-16 hours a day eating bamboo.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca.
  • Average Weight: 70-100Kgs.
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years.
  • Habitat: Cool, Wet Bamboo Forests.

The giant panda, also known as panda bear or simply panda, is a bear native to south central China. It is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body. To know more about these cute beasts, click here.
